# Remember Her — Trailer Production Prompts

## Genre & Tone
Prestige global thriller. Cold, tense, cinematic. Think: Homeland meets A Most Wanted Man.

## Logline
A humanitarian aid worker returns from a conflict zone erased from every system — while a signals decoder races to uncover a hidden transmission meant for all of humanity.

## Core Themes
- Identity erasure and institutional betrayal
- Surveillance, signals, and hidden truth
- Human cost of geopolitical shadow operations

## Visual Language
- Desaturated color grading with isolated warm tones
- Close-up emotional beats intercut with wide geopolitical establishing shots
- Signal interference overlays on key reveal moments

## Target Audience
- Premium streaming (Netflix, Apple TV+, Prime Video)
- Global thriller fans 25–54
- Investors seeking prestige IP with franchise potential

## Marketing Hook
She came back. But according to every record — she was never there.
